Ernakulam

Kochi is filled with unexpected wonders that are sure to awaken your inner explorer.

An ideal break from European structures and alleyways of Kochi would be Ernakulam, which is its twin city on the mainland. It is a modern bustling city with busy roads, commercial buildings, hotels, shopping malls and much more. An ideal way to explore Ernakulam would be to wander through the crowded Broadway or M.G. Road. The very popular Marine Drive and its walkway are one of the best places for an evening stroll. The expansive vistas of the distant Willingdon Island, the setting sun and the charming Vembanad Lake form quite an enticing frame. A sunset boat ride meandering through the Vembanad Lake is recommended to sign off the evening. There are a couple of interesting places to visit for history buffs, such has Hill Palace and its museum at Tripunithura, Kerala Folklore Museum, and Museum of Kerala History for the enthusiastic history buffs. The art gallery at Durbar Hall is a great place for art lovers as it is very often organises art exhibitions. How to Reach Ernakulam

BY ROAD

Nearest Bus Station How to reach Kochi is not a big fuss as Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) connects Kochi with all major cities in Kerala and with many cities in Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Deluxe Volvo buses, AC sleeper as well as AC normal buses are also available right from the cities to across the major destination. You can visit Thrissur (72 KM), Thiruvananthapuram (196 KM) and Madurai (231 KM) quite easily by these buses from Kochi. Bus Station(s): Eranakulam, Vyttila

BY TRAIN

: There are regular trains from other major cities of the country to Ernakulam. Railway Station(s): Ernakulam junction (south) (ERS), Ernakulam town (north) (ERN)

BY AIRLINE

Nearest Airport Nedumbassery International airport is an international airport which is situated at a distance of about 20 km from Kochi. Taxis as well as buses facilities are also available from and to the main city. There are regular flights from and to several other cities in India and also many cities in abroad, including gulf countries and Singapore.
