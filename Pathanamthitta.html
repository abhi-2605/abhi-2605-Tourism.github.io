Pathanamthitta

Welcome to headquarters of Pilgrimage Tourism

Pathanamthitta district is known as the 'Headquarters of Pilgrimage Tourism', of the Kerala State. Situated near the Western Ghats and bordered by the hills, Pathanamthitta district is a treat to eyes with its vast unending stretches of forests, rivers and rural landscapes. Blessed by nature, the district is famous for its scenic beauty, fairs and festivals. This website is the official page of District Tourism Promotion Council, Pathanamthitta (DTPC) where visitors can browse the destinations and learn about various activities of DTPC

How to Reach Pathanamthitta

BY ROAD

Nearest Bus StationThere are regular buses from other major cities of the State to Pathanamthitta. Bus Station(s): Pathanamthitta

BY TRAIN

Instead of Pathanamthitta you can a get a train to Thiruvalla or Chengannur on regular basis. 24 km away Chengannur (CNGR), kerala 30 km away Thiruvalla , kerala

BY AIRLINE

Nearest Airport Pathanamthitta does not have an airport. The nearest airport is Trivandrum International Airport (TRV) which is 103 kms from Pathanamthitta. Kochi International Airport (COK) is 123 kms away from Pathanamthitta.
